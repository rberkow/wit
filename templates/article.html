It's not that women "just aren't interested in technology and computing"


Although women are interested in technology, it still fails to attract them into studying it or working in this sector. There are many articles written on this case in discussing why women are not involved in IT as much as men are. However there are no conclusive results.


Some facts about Women in Technology:


In one study, the odds of being in a high-level position are 2.7 times greater for men than for women.

In another study, women accounted for 4% of senior management positions in technical or R&D departments. They accounted for 14% of senior management when including non-technical departments.

Women account for 9% of IT Management Positions (defined as CEO, CIO, CTO, VP, Director, Strategist, Architect)
