"Why are there so few women working in the IT and telecoms sector?"


This question taken from a Forbes article crosses everyone's mind. It is a known fact that women tend to be less active than men in the IT industry. However, the reason is unknown. Hence, in order to eliminate this question, we decided to promote women into the IT industry. Since men tend to be more involved, women either don't think of IT as an area to study, or they just tend to give up their ambition in IT.

In order to avoid this, our goal is to inspire women to study information technology. We plan to achieve this goal by arranging conferences and other informative events.

So as an organization, we hope to arrange events such as conferences and information sessions to inform McGill women on how to get involved and expand their knowledge. Furthermore, we hope to facilitate discussion and networking over this issue of women being less active in a friendly and open atmosphere where everyone will feel free to express their opinions.
